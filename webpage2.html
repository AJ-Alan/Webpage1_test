II || My Life is a Triangle.

The view is triangular,
Two points, ahead and above.
Do not gaze the hypothenuse,
It's too far, you can't reach it.

One of the legs,
It will lead to the stars.
Run toward the ends of the world,
By then, you'll be your hero.

Stargazer, that is the end,
Look up, one of the legs.
Tomorrow will not be the same,
For you, a day never passes.
